About me

Dr. Shuai Wang received the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecoms in 2011 and 2014, respectively. He received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Hong Kong in 2018. From 2018 to 2019, he has been a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Hong Kong. From 2019 to 2021, he has been a Research Assistant Professor at Southern University of Science and Technology. Currently, he is a Associate Professor with Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
