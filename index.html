About me

Dr. Shuai Wang received the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecoms in 2011 and 2014, respectively. He received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Hong Kong in 2018. From 2018 to 2019, he has been a Postdoctoral Fellow and a Lecturer at the University of Hong Kong. Currently, he is a Research Assistant Professor with Southern University of Science and Technology.

Research

My research interests include
